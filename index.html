Welcome to Hangman! In this game, you'll be guessing a hidden word one letter at a time. Each time you guess a letter, if it’s in the word, it will be revealed in its correct position. But be careful! You have a limited number of incorrect guesses—typically eleven—before the hangman is fully drawn. The goal is to guess the word before your attempts run out. So, think carefully, make your guesses, and enjoy the challenge of uncovering the mystery word! Ready to play? Let's get started!
